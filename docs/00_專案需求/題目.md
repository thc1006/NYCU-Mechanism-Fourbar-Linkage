**Topic :** 下拉式層架(A pull-down shelf)

**Goal :** Design a pull-down shelf using a four-bar mechanism to install in a cabinet hanging on the wall. The available space of the cabinet is 80 cm in height, and 40 cm in depth. The bottom of the cabinet is about 170 cm above the ground. It is required that the pull-down shelf can be installed entirely in the cabinet initially, and then the shelf can be pulled out and down to at least below 140 cm above the ground (at least 30 cm below initial height).

**Demo:** https://www.google.com/search?q=https://www.youtube.com/watch%3Fv%3D6seYi2Enm5g

Report content :  
Use your name as file name, put everything in one file (Word, PowerPoint, or pdf format, no compressed files). The first page should include the design topic, student name and ID. Following pages should include:

1. Design requirements, including constants.  
2. Design method or equations with your definitions.  
3. Design process and numerical results. Show how you decide the type of the mechanism and the link length. (State your independent and dependent variables, dimensions of your final design) What tool do you use to solve design equations if there are?  
4. Simulations result with Working model 2D. Show at least four figures for different positions, including initial and final positions. A short video showing the whole simulation movement is even better. (The video file can be a separated file in submission)  
5. Estimate available storage space (rectangular area) of the shelf. Then try **different** designs to maximize the storage space. The new design should also include at least 4 figures at different positions from Working model 2D simulation results.  
6. Conclusion (Summarize your results) and Discussions. (Such as advantages and disadvantages of your results. What can you do to maximize the storage space? Difficulties during this project and how do you solve them ? What do you learn or feel after this project.)

## **6\. 深度分析**

這份文件是一項佔總成績 14% 的機構設計專案作業，要求學生設計一個安裝在壁櫥內的下拉式層架。

1. **核心挑戰 (The Problem):**  
   * **機制:** 必須使用「四連桿機構」(four-bar mechanism)。  
   * **空間限制:** 櫥櫃空間僅有 80cm (高) x 40cm (深)。層架初始時必須「完全收納」在此空間內。  
   * **運動要求:** 層架需要能從 170cm 的初始高度，「拉出並下降」到至少 140cm 的高度（即至少下降 30cm）。  
2. **關鍵交付成果 (Deliverables):**  
   * **一份報告 (Report):** 需包含設計要求、方法（方程式）、設計過程（如何決定連桿長度、變數等）、模擬結果、儲存空間分析、結論與討論。  
   * **模擬 (Simulation):**  
     * 必須使用 "Working model 2D" 軟體。  
     * 報告中至少要附上 4 張不同位置的截圖。  
     * 手寫筆記「模擬 results video or 4張圖」再次確認了這一點，並表明提交影片（a short video）是更好（even better）的選項。  
     * 手寫筆記「\# work file preferred」暗示教授可能也希望看到 Working Model 的原始工作檔案。  
   * **最佳化 (Optimization):**  
     * 第 5 點要求不只做一個設計，而是要「嘗試不同設計 (try different designs)」來「最大化儲存空間 (maximize the storage space)」。  
     * "different" 一詞被特別用螢光筆劃記，顯示這是評分的關鍵步驟，學生必須展現迭代和優化設計的能力。  
3. **手寫筆記的意涵:**  
   * 「大概要花一週」: 表明這份專案的工作量不小，預計需要一週時間完成。  
   * 「記得分段寫, 寫完整」: 提醒學生報告的第 2 點（設計方法與方程式）要有條理且內容完整。  
   * 「流程要清楚\!」: 強調第 3 點（設計過程）的論述必須邏輯清晰，讓閱讀者能明白設計的來龍去脈。  
4. 總結:  
   這不只是一個單純的設計題，它是一個完整的工程專案。學生需要結合理論（機構學、運動學方程式）、工具（Working model 2D 模擬）和實務（在嚴格的空間限制下達成運動目標），並透過迭代設計來尋求最佳解（最大化儲存空間）。
